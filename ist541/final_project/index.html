Teaching Kids to Read with Phonics




Welcome!

Reading is very important. When children are young, we should provide them with a good reading environment, teach them how to read.

The goal of phonics instruction is to help children learn the alphabetic principle — the idea that letters represent the sounds of spoken language — and that there is an organized, logical, and predictable relationship between written letters and spoken sounds.

This webpage is to help you understand phonics, learn the alphabetic principle. And you can help your students to read with phonics.
